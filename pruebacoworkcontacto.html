Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about tours and memberships

What should I expect during a tour?

Our tours typically last 30-45 minutes and include a walkthrough of our facilities, introduction to available amenities, viewing of workspace options that match your needs, and a Q&A session with our community manager. You'll also receive a complimentary day pass to try our space.

How do I choose the right location for my needs?

Each location has unique advantages: Zone 10 is ideal for finance professionals, Zone 14 offers proximity to embassies and corporate services, while Zone 16 provides a more lifestyle-oriented environment with weekend access. Our team can help you select the best fit based on your work patterns, client meetings, and team requirements.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

All Nexo Space locations follow strict health protocols including enhanced cleaning, HEPA air filtration, sanitization stations, spaced seating arrangements, and mandatory mask policies in common areas. Private offices have individual ventilation controls. Virtual tours are also available if you prefer to explore remotely.

Can I bring my team for the tour?

Absolutely! We encourage you to bring key team members who will be using the space. For teams larger than 5 people, please let us know in advance so we can ensure a team-focused tour experience. We can also arrange team-specific demonstrations of collaborative tools and meeting spaces.

What happens after I book a tour?

After submitting your tour request, you'll receive a confirmation email within 2 hours. Our community manager will contact you within 24 hours to confirm details and answer preliminary questions. Before your tour, we'll send a reminder with directions, parking information, and safety protocols.
